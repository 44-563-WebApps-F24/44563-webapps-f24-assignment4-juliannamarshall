Julianna Marshall presents a Greek play by Sophocles

Antigone by Sophocles

ISM. Unhappy one! But what can I herein Avail to do or undo? ANT. Wilt thou share The danger and the labour? Make thy choice. ISM. Of what wild enterprise? What canst thou mean? ANT. Wilt thou join hand with mine to lift the dead? ISM. To bury him, when all have been forbidden? Is that thy thought? ANT. To bury my own brother And thine, even though thou wilt not do thy part. I will not be a traitress to my kin. ISM. Fool-hardy girl! against the word of Creon?
